Internal AI Usage Policy Reference

Employees may use approved local AI tools for drafting, summarization, classification, and retrieval over approved internal documents. They must review outputs before use and remain responsible for final decisions.

Restricted information, including payment card data, government identification numbers, patient records, secrets, and unreleased financial results, must not be entered into unapproved AI systems.

AI-generated recommendations cannot be the sole basis for legal, medical, employment, credit, or security access decisions. Human review and source verification are required.
