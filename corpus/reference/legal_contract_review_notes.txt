Legal Contract Review Notes Reference

The standard services agreement limits aggregate liability to fees paid in the prior twelve months. Any request for uncapped liability must be escalated to Legal and Finance.

Data processing terms require subprocessors to be listed in the vendor portal before customer data is shared. Security addenda must include breach notification within seventy-two hours after confirmation.

Non-standard indemnity language is acceptable only when the business owner documents the commercial rationale and Legal approves the final clause.
