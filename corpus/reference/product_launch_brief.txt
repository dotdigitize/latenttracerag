Product Launch Brief Reference

The Orion Insights launch targets operations leaders who need queue analytics, exception trend reporting, and team workload visibility. The launch date is planned for 2026-05-20.

The first release includes dashboard templates, scheduled PDF exports, and integration with Atlas Workflow. It does not include predictive staffing recommendations.

Launch readiness depends on documentation signoff, security review completion, customer support training, and a rollback plan for the Atlas integration.
